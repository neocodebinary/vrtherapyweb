App Support

If you are experiencing issues with VR Mirror Therapy, need help with installation, or have feature suggestions, our team is here to help.

✉️ vrmirrortherapy@gmail.com
Why is the screen black? Please ensure you have granted Camera Permissions in your device settings. The app needs the camera to create the mirror effect.
Do I need a VR Headset? Yes, for the full therapeutic experience, insert your phone into a standard mobile VR viewer (like Google Cardboard).
Is it free? Yes, the app is 100% free and Open Source. No hidden costs.
